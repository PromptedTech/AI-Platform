============================================
OPTION 1: Deploy via Vercel Dashboard (Easiest - No CLI needed)
============================================

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/new

2. Connect your GitHub account (if you have one)
   OR
   Upload your project as a ZIP

3. Click "Import Project"

4. In the "Environment Variables" section, add these:

   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_API_KEY=your_value
   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_AUTH_DOMAIN=your_value
   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_PROJECT_ID=your_value
   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_STORAGE_BUCKET=your_value
   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_MESSAGING_SENDER_ID=your_value
   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_APP_ID=your_value
   NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_MEASUREMENT_ID=your_value
   OPENAI_API_KEY=your_openai_key
   RAZORPAY_KEY_ID=your_razorpay_key
   RAZORPAY_KEY_SECRET=your_razorpay_secret
   NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL=https://your-project.vercel.app

5. Click "Deploy"

6. Wait 2-3 minutes

7. Your site is LIVE! ðŸŽ‰

============================================
OPTION 2: Deploy via CLI (What you just tried)
============================================

Step 1: Login to Vercel
   Run: vercel login

Step 2: Deploy
   Run: vercel --prod

Step 3: Follow the prompts:
   - Link to existing project? N
   - Project name? ai-platform (or your choice)
   - Directory? ./ (just press Enter)
